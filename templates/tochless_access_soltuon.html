Intrusion And Touchless Access Solutions
Detect and Deter intruders from your perimeter. Intelligent intrusion detection is a delicate balance between responding to real security breaches and ignoring costly false alarm sources. The advanced sensors provide best-in-class performance while virtually eliminating false triggers such as strong drafts, moving objects, and the presence of pets.



Intruder Perimeter Detection
A perimeter intrusion detection system (PIDS) is a device or sensor that detects the presence of an intruder attempting to breach the physical perimeter of a property, building, or other secured area.A Perimeter Intruder Detection System is typically deployed as part of an overall security system and is often found in high-security environments such as Central Jail, Airports,Military bases, and Nuclear plants. Types of Perimeter Detection solutions broadly classified:

  • Active IR
  • Break Wire
  • Electrostatic Field
  • Fence Detection
  • Ground Motion
  • Magnetic
  • Microwave Detectors


Power Fencing
Power Fencing is another solution that can be deployed to prevent intruders getting into your premises.
  • Short pulse of high voltage is passed through the fence on regular intervals or when someone tries to pass over the fence.
  • When the intruder touches the fence,he/she/any animal immediately retracts and avoids.
  • Detect & deter unauthorized entries.


Door Closers
A door closer is an adjustable and spring-loaded mechanical arm that closes a door automatically after it has been opened.There are types of door closers which are better suited to manual doors, automatic doors, and electromagnetic swing varieties. The right door closer size also influences the efficiency of the door closer. If you choose an incorrect size, it will be difficult to operate the door. Purpose of a Door Closer are:

  • Ensure fire doors are closed.
  • Prevents the slamming of doors
  • Controls the open swing of doors
  • Adds security through door closer


Electromechanical And Mechanical Locks
Electronic lockers are keyless entries with advanced security features.They can be controlled through mobile apps, cards, thumb impression, lock pin numbers etc.

  • Available for various door thicknesses. Several models and varieties to choose from.
  • Wired and Wireless models.
  • Tracks data on entry, exit or failed attempts.
  • Locks can be integrated with other physical security systems.
  • Keyless entry, some examples could be keypad and codes, electronic cards including magstripe, proximity and smart cards, and biometrics including fingerprint, facial characteristics, and retina scans.
  • Mechanical override, in case of electronic failure in the lock
  • Gives you higher control like time and date range specific, can be controlled remotely.
